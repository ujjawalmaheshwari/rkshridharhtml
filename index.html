RK Sridhar

RK Sridhar (Sridhar Krishnappa) is a young serial entrepreneur and social activist from Bengaluru. His journey as a young activist and changemaker has been brief but with impact truly demolishing the prejudice that age is considered in the Indian society. Always standing up against the oppression of any kind, Sridhar’s ideology is one - a stronger, independent and sustainable India whom he loves because of his instinct and worships because of his understanding of India. Quoting Sridhar, “I loved my nation as a young boy because of my nationalist attitude but while working for my motherland and knowing her with my experience I have started worshipping her”. For Sridhar there exists one and only one blockage in between ideas and actions, and he defines the blockage as an internal stigma and nothing else.

Early Days:

Sridhar is born and brought up in Bengaluru, the capital of Karnataka. Belonging to the Thigala Community, culture and Hinduism became an internal part of Sridhar’s life. Living the epic Mahabharata with the prestigious traditions like ‘Karga’ he learnt from the boyhood that the Indian traditions were woven around the grand revolution called Vedas fitted in every way for every sect and school of thoughts propagating a natural democracy among the people. Naturally becoming a Shakta - worshipper of Adi Shakthi, Sridhar never refrained from believing in the ideology of Shaivas and Vaishnavas equally.

Unity in diversity became a part and parcel of his everyday life. Done with schooling and graduating from an academic brand like Baldwin Sridhar had lesser beliefs in the conventional systems and willed to redefine the existing systems with his zeal. During his college days he entered the realms of college politics while winning the local elections without any backgrounds or party-worship as his popularity gained the acceleration because of his idealistic nature and being there for all attitude

One Goal: Upliftment of Society

RK Sridhar had one goal from the beginning, to work against oppression and uplift the masses around. Though struggling with his venture already, Sridhar decided to start off upon the path he had envisioned forever: to work for one India and the greatest India.

Loves Children

He started visiting orphanages and old age homes near him and started contributing nearly 20% of his earnings from his venture for the sustainable growth of the organisations working for the underprivileged. Though it was tough for him to match his own ends, he never ceased his beliefs.

Youth Found Their New Leader

Gradually his dedication started getting recognized and youngsters of his area started coming together to work with him. He adopted the ways of Shaheed-E-Azam Bhagat Singh and Gandhian ways combined to get in touch with the local masses to fight against oppression while practising Ahimsa and mass movements as solutions.

The Voices of Unheard Given Importance

Karnataka and Kannadigas for the first time saw a backbone working for them and their causes. Quoting the organization, “Putting you in control so that you can make informed choices”, the mission, vision and work fell in line. The youth saw for the first time the voice of the average masses getting heard, Karnataka saw interventions on subjects of national importance 2007-08 onwards

The Vision

Sridhar understood that the path ahead can be forged by working at the ground level, working for as well as with the masses of Karnataka with organisational support. Many young men and women had become full-time volunteers dedicating a part of their lives at the grass-root level and hence started a new chapter in his life.

Jaya Karnataka

Joining Jaya Karnataka Sridhar had one aim, to cut down all the boundaries and rise as a ‘people’s worker’ independent of problems. He started travelling and staying in the remote places of Karnataka to understand the people, their problems and feasibility of the solutions at hand. His dedicated yet pragmatic attitude accompanied with the zeal to solve the problems started getting recognized in the ranks of the organization and his ability to lead got accepted by everyone.

Sridhar had always been affected by the problems being faced by the feeders of the nation - the farmers of the nation. Clearly, Karnataka faces the biggest challenges when it comes to the irrigation availabilities and scope to better the same. Mother Cauvery and Mother Mahadayi being 2 of the life sources, both face a perennial struggle with the neighbouring states Tamil Nadu and Goa. Sridhar knew that the betterment will happen only when the farmers are represented well at the national level with Civil Participation in the movement. The years in between 2016-18 saw a growing public agitation against the issue and Sridhar decided to peacefully agitate against the sufferings of the farmers by bringing together the youth.

Eventually, the organization decides to give Sridhar the reposnibility as the Vice President of Bengaluru District. As a people’s person, he didn’t want to come under the limelight but when the high command insisted he saw it as a way to practise his beliefs better and go forth. The years hence on saw many activities from Sridhar’s side including civil non-cooperation movement against the Cauvery issue in 2016.

More than thousands of young activists gathered under the leadership of Sridhar to reflect the problems of the farmers trying to get public attention nationally. A democratic revolution breakthrough happened and nationwide support got recognized. Hence on Sridhar got attached more with farmers’ sentiment and decided to never stop. Mahadayi had been anothe issue which needed the organisational support and this time Sridhar did his part more effectively. In no time he assumed the position of the national Secretary of Jaya Karnataka, all due to the effective and iterative ground work he did by listening and representing the farmers. In 2017, a first of its kind national representation got made on the Mahadayi issue.

Many other issues were duly tackled ranging from the employability of the youth to the cultural void that Karnataka had been suffering from at the national level under the able leadership of RK Sridhar. Many youngsters around the state started connecting with him, nearly 10,000 youngsters started following the national and social shown by Jaya Karnataka and RK Sridhar.

Many non-identified issues didn’t only get identified but solved in the period including a humanitarian issue like of Malai Mahadeshwara in 2019 where lack of natural water supply had become a question of life and death for the animals in the area.

Artificial ponds got made with proper water supply to quench the thirst of the animals. Understanding the necessity that water is, Sridhar developed another aspiration to provide water to the needed, animals or humans, at least.

Hence started another movement of supplying water to the needy all across Karnataka, especially around Bengaluru.

Entrepreneurial Days:

From here Sridhar decided to define his own destiny. Special influences came form greats like Swami Vivekananda and Dr APJ Abdul Kalam, a lifetime decision of becoming a job creator became concrete. Having seen and fancied the buildings around him and understanding that infrastructural growth means development, Sridhar decided to try and succeed as a real estate entrepreneur. Starting his career by assisting minor contractors to try to understand the business by standing and learning at construction sites, 2 to 3 years passed by moulding a young boy into a businessman.

Minor steps create great stories and hence followed the never-ending growth of RKS Builders with many futuristic buildings and layouts bearing the brand built by a young man. True that struggles came into the picture but how have they barred a hard worker ever in the history? A prestigious and trusted brand which has seen all the thicks and thins, RKS Builders has emerged as a synonym to Growing India.

Hejje:

It was the need of the hour to go more full-fledged and it was only possible with an entrepreneurial move. A step to start off with to achieve the same Sridhar founded Hejje in July 2020. Gathering together the youth and fellow Social Activists, Hejje got registered in August 2020. Yet to start its operations formally, Hejje started off a campaign called Amar Parva to connect with the families of martyrs and veterans from the Indian Armed Forces. Nearly 15 families have been connceted with within a month.

Also, the disrupted education system received an attention from Team Hejje and a few solutions are being structured and executed to uplift the academic zeal and the spirit of education. Further, the plans are too revamp existing Government schools of Karnataka to attract the youth.

Though young Hejje has the ambitious plans to become the voice of the voiceless at every front. Independent of associations, ideologies and inclinations - Hejje aspires to stand one and always with Karnataka and India.

A Journey, Forever:

It has been a journey started yet an earning received for RK Sridhar and he has no plans to slow down. Almost entering his early 30s Sridhar visualizes a majestic future for the people and has vowed to dedicate his life in service. Entrepreneurial and Industrial movements to increase the GDP of India remains one of his visions for now. He also wishes to work constantly to make the last citizen belief that he/she is the future of India.
